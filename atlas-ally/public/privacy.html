Privacy Policy

Atlas Ally LLC  ·  Effective Date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Atlas Ally LLC ("Atlas Ally," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our travel safety platform at atlas-ally.com (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name
  • Date of birth (to verify you are 18 or older)
  • WhatsApp phone number (used for login and safety alerts)
  • Email address (optional)
  • State and country of origin (optional)
  • Emergency contact names and WhatsApp numbers
  • Invite codes used during registration

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • GPS location data — only when you enable Journey Mode or request safety scoring. We do not continuously track your location in the background without your knowledge.
  • Device type and browser (used to optimize the app experience)
  • Pages visited and features used within the app
  • Incidents and reports you submit to the platform
  • Feedback and ratings you submit

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Weather data from Open-Meteo (no personal data shared)
  • News content from Google News RSS feeds (no personal data shared)
  • Payment information processed by Stripe (we never see or store your card number)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and maintain your account
  • Send you WhatsApp safety alerts for countries you are monitoring
  • Notify your emergency contacts when you use the I'm Safe check-in
  • Calculate real-time safety scores based on your GPS location
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Improve the accuracy and relevance of safety information
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Twilio — to send WhatsApp verification codes and safety alerts
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments
  • Railway — our hosting provider in the United States

3.2 Emergency Situations

If you use the I'm Safe feature or trigger a geofence alert, your name, location, and status are sent to the emergency contacts you have designated.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • OTP codes expire after 10 minutes and are purged automatically
  • Backup copies may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at support@atlas-ally.com.

You can also delete your account directly from the app: open the hamburger menu → Account → Delete My Account.

6. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • No passwords — authentication uses WhatsApp OTP codes only
  • JWT tokens expire after 30 days
  • Database is backed up daily

7. Children's Privacy

Atlas Ally is not directed to children under 18. We require all users to confirm they are 18 or older during registration. If we discover an underage user, we will delete their account promptly.

8. International Users

Atlas Ally is operated from Texas, United States. If you are in the European Union, we rely on your consent as the legal basis for processing your personal data. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Atlas Ally does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. We use browser localStorage to store your session token and preferences only.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you via WhatsApp of material changes at least 14 days before they take effect.

11. Contact Us
